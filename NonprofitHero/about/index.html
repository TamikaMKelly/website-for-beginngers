What is Nonprofit Hero about?

Lazandra Dial, CEO
Lazandra is a Consultant, Speaker and Author with over 20 years of experience of supporting the Nonprofit industry .
She is recognized and known for her expertise in helping them secure government funding, strategic planning, and organizational development.

Lazandra is a proud alumnus of California State University Hayward, where she received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Sociology. Following graduation Lazandra began her career at a nonprofit based in Oakland, CA and through the years held various positions with the State of California, Alameda County and the City of Oakland.
Today Lazandra helps nonprofit leaders and their teams make their vision and mission fundable through one-on-one consulting, coaching, and training.
